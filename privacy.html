Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

What we collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping address

Payment information (card number, expiration, CVC) is collected and processed entirely by Stripe. We never see, store, or have access to your payment details.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to fulfill your order and communicate with you about your purchase. That is it.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your information to third parties
  • We do not share your information with advertisers
  • We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in
  • We do not use tracking pixels or retargeting cookies

Third-party services

We use the following services to operate this store:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS, email routing, and serverless functions (cloudflare.com/privacypolicy)

Cookies

This site uses localStorage to remember your cart and audio preferences. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing hello@acculoe.com.

California residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
